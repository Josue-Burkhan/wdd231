Wild Fantasy -Writers

Site Name

The site is named Wild Fantasy because it reflects the endless creative possibilities within every writer and conveys a sense of bold, imaginative expression.

Site Purpose

The website is designed to be a platform where writers can join an exclusive community. Members enjoy benefits such as access to writing workshops, networking opportunities, and collaborative projects, all aimed at nurturing their craft and expanding their creative horizons.

Scenarios

  • What benefits do I receive by joining Wild Fantasy as a writer?
  • What is the process to get accepted into Wild Fantasy?
  • How is the content I publish on the site promoted?
  • Is there any cost associated with becoming a member?
  • What types of works are accepted or published on Wild Fantasy?

Wireframes

  • A form that includes fields such as name, a short biography, your writing genres, and a link to your portfolio.
  • Recent Publications Grid: A grid layout displaying the published works by writers, along with a short synopsis and a “read more” button.
  • Registration Page: A simple registration form for new users who wish to join the site, including an option to subscribe to the newsletter.
  • Footer with Contact Information: A footer that includes links to privacy policies, direct contact information, and social media channels.
